What is Ame ?

AME is responsible to ensure that the aircrafts is operating properly or not. The safety, security and maintenance of aircrafts and its passengers are on the shoulders of Aircraft Maintenance Engineer. AME inspects, services, repairs and overhauls civil aircrafts..

As Air India releases new rules, airline to deem crew unhealthy to fly for 18 months as permanently unfit
